Applied Instruments is a trusted place for importing and wholesaling all kinds of Scientific Laboratory Instruments, Glassware, Chemical, Electronic Digital Weighing Scales. We have been in business since 1994 till now. Alamgir Traders and Abrar Traders was the former name and now we have set sail in a new domain as Applied Instruments. We are specialized in importing, wholesaling and after sales services of instruments in the fields of washing, dying, textiles, garments, pharmaceuticals, agricultural research and food industries, soil surveying, all kinds of weighing solutions, water quality control and measurements, environmental treatment plants (ETP) and any kinds of special research and development laboratory equipment from well-known companies such as Clean Instruments, Fawag, Gondo Instruments, Nicety, Smart Sensor, Trans Instruments and others.
